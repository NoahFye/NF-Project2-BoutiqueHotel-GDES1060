These are the philosophies we strive to uphold here at the Mercurial Health and Fitness Inn. We aim to provide our guests with the best hotel experience possible for the mind and body, with a holistic focus on inspiring and developing healthy habits for years to come.

The Inn was created in partnership by a former D1 strength and conditioning coach at Loyola University, and a Registered Dietician Nutritionist at Johns Hopkins Hospital, and serves the greater Northeast and Mid-Atlantic regions’ most dedicated athletes.

This is not a hotel that you will laze by the pool at, instead, you will swim laps until you are certain you can achieve the mental will to complete an Ironman marathon. Guests will begin their stay with an ideal wellness persona or dream in mind, and will be well on their way to getting there before their visit is over thanks to our personal trainers chomping at the bit to see you succeed through hard work and lifestyle changes.
